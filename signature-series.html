The Signature Series operates outside Deco Preda's standard specification framework. Each commission begins with a material brief — a description of intended environment, light condition, adjacent materials and desired character — and develops through a structured sampling protocol before a single metre of surface is applied.

The compositions shown here are documented outcomes of completed commissions. They are presented as reference — not as options. Your commission begins from a blank material specification, not from a selection of existing finishes.

The commission
process.

01

Material brief

You describe the space, the light conditions and the character you are after. We translate this into a technical material framework before any sampling begins.

02

Sampling protocol

We develop physical test panels — typically two to three rounds — until the material matches the brief. Nothing progresses to installation without written approval of an approved sample.

03

Application

The same team that developed your sample applies your surface. No hand-offs. Application is documented at every stage against the approved specification.

04

Documentation

At practical completion you receive a full technical handover: material certificates, application records and maintenance specification. The surface is warranted.
