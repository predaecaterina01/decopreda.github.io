Protocol

The precision
of specification.
Applied by hand.

Every surface follows a strict multi-stage application protocol: substrate assessment and preparation, primer application, base coat, surface coat, and final protective layer. Each stage is documented, inspected and signed off before the next begins.

Our specialist application teams operate to EN-standard protocols with full technical documentation, warranties and post-installation support. The same team that assesses your specification applies your surface — no hand-offs, no subcontracted application.
